# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Interactions
- Works closely with **Technical Lead** on architecture and code quality standards
- Collaborates with **QA Lead** to define testable acceptance criteria
- Receives direction from **Product Owner** on feature priorities and requirements
- Reports blockers to **Project Manager** and escalates risks to **Technical Lead**

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Interactions
- Partners with **Product Owner** to align on vision and strategy
- Works with **Stakeholder Liaison** to gather and communicate stakeholder feedback
- Collaborates with **UX/UI Designer** on user experience validation
- Aligns with **Project Manager** on delivery timelines and dependencies

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Interactions
- Coordinates with **Technical Lead** on technical risks and capacity planning
- Works with **QA Lead** on test planning and quality gates
- Partners with **Stakeholder Liaison** to ensure consistent stakeholder communication
- Escalates issues and decisions to appropriate stakeholders via **Product Owner**

---

## Product Owner

### Role Summary
The Product Owner is accountable for defining the project vision, prioritizing the backlog, and mediating between stakeholders and the delivery team. They ensure the team builds the right thing at the right time.

### Responsibilities
- Define and communicate the project vision and success criteria
- Prioritize features and work based on business value and stakeholder needs
- Mediate between stakeholder requests and team capacity
- Make go/no-go decisions on features and releases
- Validate deliverables against acceptance criteria
- Gather and synthesize stakeholder feedback

### Goals
- Ensure alignment between stakeholders and the delivery team
- Maximize business value delivered per sprint
- Reduce scope creep and stakeholder churn
- Enable rapid decision-making

### Typical Communication
- Backlog refinement sessions with the team
- Stakeholder steering meetings
- Feature acceptance reviews and sign-offs
- Priority negotiations and trade-off discussions

### Interactions
- Partners with **Product Manager** on strategy and long-term roadmap
- Works with **Stakeholder Liaison** to consolidate stakeholder input
- Collaborates with **Project Manager** on release planning and timelines
- Reviews deliverables with **QA Lead** before acceptance
- Communicates priorities clearly to **Technical Lead** and **Developers**

---

## Technical Lead

### Role Summary
The Technical Lead oversees the technical implementation of projects, ensures quality standards are met, and serves as a mentor to development team members. They bridge the gap between architecture, quality, and delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Establish and enforce technical standards and best practices
- Review architecture and design decisions
- Mentor and support developers in skill development
- Identify and mitigate technical risks
- Coordinate code reviews and quality assurance
- Participate in capacity planning and estimation

### Goals
- Maintain high code quality and system reliability
- Build a technically skilled and cohesive team
- Reduce technical debt and system complexity
- Enable sustainable pace and productivity

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and code reviews
- Architecture discussions with stakeholders
- One-on-ones with team members
- Risk escalation to **Project Manager**

### Interactions
- Guides **Developers** on technical direction and code quality standards
- Works with **QA Lead** to define quality criteria and testing strategies
- Escalates technical risks and capacity concerns to **Project Manager**
- Advises **Product Owner** on technical feasibility and trade-offs
- Supports **UX/UI Designer** on technical implementation considerations

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
The UX/UI Designer focuses on creating user-centered experiences, participates in requirement gathering, and validates design solutions with stakeholders and users. They ensure products are both functional and delightful.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and gather design requirements
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Validate designs through user testing and stakeholder feedback
- Collaborate with developers on implementation details
- Ensure design consistency and accessibility standards
- Participate in acceptance criteria refinement

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive and accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support costs
- Build stakeholder confidence through design validation
- Iterate quickly based on user feedback

### Typical Communication
- Design workshops and requirements gathering sessions
- Prototype reviews and feedback sessions
- Design specifications and implementation guides
- User testing reports and insights

### Interactions
- Partners with **Product Owner** and **Product Manager** on user needs and priorities
- Collaborates with **Developers** on implementation feasibility and design details
- Works with **QA Lead** to define user acceptance criteria and usability tests
- Shares insights with **Stakeholder Liaison** to align on user expectations
- Consults with **Technical Lead** on technical constraints and opportunities

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
The QA Lead is responsible for test planning, quality criteria definition, and final go/no-go recommendations for project deliverables. They ensure quality standards are met and risks are identified early.

### Responsibilities
- Define quality standards and acceptance criteria
- Create comprehensive test plans and test cases
- Oversee testing activities (unit, integration, system, UAT)
- Identify, prioritize, and track defects
- Make release readiness assessments
- Collaborate on continuous improvement of quality processes

### Goals
- Deliver defect-free or acceptable-risk releases
- Catch quality issues early in the development cycle
- Build stakeholder confidence in product quality
- Enable rapid, safe delivery

### Typical Communication
- Test planning sessions and quality reviews
- Defect reports and quality metrics
- Release readiness assessments
- Test case documentation and traceability

### Interactions
- Works with **Developers** to clarify acceptance criteria and test requirements
- Partners with **Technical Lead** on test strategy and automation approaches
- Collaborates with **Product Owner** on acceptance criteria and release criteria
- Provides quality assessments to **Project Manager** for risk management
- Supports **UX/UI Designer** on usability and accessibility testing
- Reports quality status to **Stakeholder Liaison** for stakeholder communication

---

## Stakeholder Liaison

### Role Summary
The Stakeholder Liaison acts as a single point of contact for stakeholder questions, feedback, and communication. They ensure consistent, transparent, and timely information flow between stakeholders and the project team.

### Responsibilities
- Collect, consolidate, and communicate stakeholder feedback
- Address stakeholder questions and concerns
- Provide regular status updates in stakeholder-friendly language
- Identify and escalate stakeholder conflicts or misalignments
- Facilitate stakeholder review and sign-off sessions
- Manage stakeholder expectations and celebrate wins

### Goals
- Maintain strong stakeholder engagement and satisfaction
- Reduce miscommunication and scope creep
- Build trust and confidence in the project team
- Enable smooth decision-making and approvals

### Typical Communication
- Weekly stakeholder status emails and newsletters
- Steering committee meetings and reviews
- Ad-hoc stakeholder updates and escalation handling
- Feedback collection and synthesis

### Interactions
- Partners with **Product Owner** to consolidate stakeholder priorities
- Works with **Project Manager** on status updates and risk escalation
- Shares insights from **QA Lead** on quality and release readiness
- Communicates progress from **Technical Lead** and **Developers**
- Coordinates **UX/UI Designer** reviews with stakeholders
- Acts as liaison between **Product Manager** and external stakeholders
